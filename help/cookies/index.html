What are 'cookies'?

Cookies are small bits of data that pages can set, often used to keep you signed in or remember that you passed a captcha, perhaps.

How does storytime use 'cookies'?

StoryTime only ever uses cookies to remember that you've signed in - stored as a cookie called signedin=true, which never expires.
